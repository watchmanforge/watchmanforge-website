Features

One App.
Total Control.

Stop juggling multiple brand apps. The ChromaForge connects to all your LED hardware through a single, powerful interface.

Multi-Brand Support

Connect to any Bluetooth LED controller across brands. Our universal protocol layer bridges the gaps left by manufacturer apps.

Scene Programming

Build custom light sequences with a visual timeline editor. Program color transitions, strobes, and breathing patterns with precision timing.

Music Sync

Let your LEDs react to music in real time. Beat detection drives color changes and intensity for a synchronized audio-visual experience.

Preset Library

Dozens of professionally designed presets ready to use. Save your own creations and share them with the enthusiast community.

Zone Control

Control different zones of your vehicle independently. Front, rear, interior, underglow — each with its own color and pattern settings.

Timer & Auto-Off

Schedule LEDs to turn on at dusk and off at dawn. Set timers, auto-off durations, and Bluetooth range-based triggers.
